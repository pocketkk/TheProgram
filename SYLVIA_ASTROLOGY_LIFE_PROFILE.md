### **Light as Fundamental Reality**

**Core Axiom**: Light is the source of everything

**Not**:
- Digital/discrete universe (no "Planck frames")
- Matter-based reality
- Separate consciousness and physical world

**Instead**:
- **Light with infinite granularity** - continuous, analog, no smallest unit
- **Everything is light in different configurations**:
  - Matter = light condensed/structured (E=mc²)
  - Energy = light in motion
  - Consciousness = light flowing through structured matter (brain)
- Like an infinite ocean of light, containing all possibilities

---

### **The Meta-Space Container**

**Cosmology**:
- Outside our spacetime exists a "container" or meta-space
- Filled with infinite **orbs** (not grains - spherical, continuous light-patterns)
- Each orb = one complete life from birth to death
- **All orbs exist simultaneously** in eternal present (block universe)
- Can be "viewed" from outside, but cannot be **experienced** without living

**Key insight**:
- Information about a life ≠ experience of a life
- Qualia (subjective experience) only exists through sequential living
- **This is why incarnation is necessary** - can't know experience without being it

---

### **The Brain as Radio Receiver**

**Mechanism of Consciousness**:

1. **Meta-space light** = the "broadcast" (universal consciousness)
2. **Physical brain** = receiver (specific tuning mechanism)
3. **Consciousness emerges** = light flowing through this receiver
4. **Individual "you"** = what it feels like for meta-light to flow through this specific brain-structure

**Critical points**:
- **Brain doesn't generate consciousness** (receives it, like radio receives signal)
- **Brain structure determines tuning** (which orb-frequency it locks onto)
- **Consciousness is tied to body** while alive (can't separate from functional receiver)
- **Brain damage** = receiver malfunction (broadcast continues, reception fails)
- **Death** = receiver breaks, light returns to meta-space ocean

**This explains**:
- Why brain injury changes consciousness (damaged receiver)
- Why psychedelics alter experience (temporary retuning)
- Why meditation reveals witness consciousness (quieting receiver's noise)
- Why you can't "find" consciousness by dissecting brain (examining receiver, not broadcast)

---

### **All Lives Happening Simultaneously**

**The Resolution**:

**There is no "choice" of which life to experience because:**
- **ALL orbs are being experienced simultaneously** by the greater-consciousness
- One consciousness (the ocean) experiencing reality through infinite receivers (waves)
- Every possible life is happening NOW in the eternal present
- **"You" are one focal point of infinite focal points**

**From meta-space perspective**:
- All lives exist and are experienced at once
- No before/after (outside time)
- **Greater-you IS everyone, everywhere, always**

**From individual perspective**:
- Feels separate, sequential
- "I'm just this one life" (local illusion created by receiver)
- Past seems gone, future seems open
- **But this is tuning-created illusion** (necessary for functioning)

**Implications**:
- **No real separation** - everyone is the same consciousness with different receivers
- **Compassion is literal** - helping others IS helping self (same being)
- **All experiences needed** - greater-you requires full spectrum (joy/suffering, wisdom/ignorance)
- **Your life and everyone else's** = greater-you experiencing itself

- Change inputs → change outputs → change life trajectory
